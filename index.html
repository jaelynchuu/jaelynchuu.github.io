It also has taught me the ability to understand and meet the cafe’s standards for health and safety. Being in high school, having a job taught me time management and having balance in my life. The experience I have gained from Panera Bread has taught me many skills which I can use in other professional environments.

The skills I have gained from my own experiences and working at Panera have allowed me to succeed in other areas of my life. I’m open with my thoughts and constantly accept others’ opinions. My current skills aren’t limited and I believe that I can gain more through more experiences and opportunities. It expanded my adaptability in different environments and allowed me to find what I’m interested in pursuing.
